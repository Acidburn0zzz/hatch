What is #VizLou?

#VizLou is a place to share what you love about Louisville, and your ideas to make it better.

All visions shared here will contribute to Vision Louisville, a community process to shape the look, feel and flow of Louisville in the next 25 years. The Vision will help create a strategic framework of major goals and specific projects that will shape the built environment throughout the entire community.

Allies are listening.

Led by Mayor Greg Fischer, Allies are community leaders using #VizLou to hear ideas for the future of Louisville. They're engaging with visionary youth by reading and sharing visions, clicking to show support, and adding comments to help visions become better defined.

Are you a community leader? City official? Educator? Pastor? Mentor? Sign up to become an Ally!
