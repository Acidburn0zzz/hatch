What is VizLou?

VizLou is a place to share what you love about Louisville, and your ideas (visions) to make it better. Want to share something you love about Louisville? Tweet with the hashtag #vizlou. Have a great idea for the future of Louisville? Add it here!

Allies are the community leaders, city officials, mentors, and educators listening to your ideas for the future of Louisville.
