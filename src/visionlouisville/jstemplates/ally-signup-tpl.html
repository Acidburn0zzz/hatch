#VizLou is a website for young adults to share their visions for the future of Louisville.

Your participation is vital: promote the tool and use it to engage with youth. Allies like you can can engage and empower young adults by engaging, sharing and supporting their visions. Your comments and suggestions help develop visions and encourage the visionaries. Thanks for your time!

To get started sign in with your Twitter account and register as an ally.
Two ways to engage

Allies help get the conversation started by sharing inspirations, and they comment, heart and share visions. #VizLou is live until August 15th, check in frequently to see the latest.

Share inspirations

What can you inspire? Tweet a positive story about the West End:
Grilling and chilling on my block. That’s why I love the ‘Ville. #vizlou
#vizlou watching high schoolers graduate! So proud

Include a pic! Use the #vizlou hashtag. It will show up on VizLou.org, where visionaries can respond to it.

  • Read visions.
  • Click the heart to show support for visions.
  • Share visions using your Twitter account, to demonstrate your interest and to draw in others.
  • Comment to help visions become better defined.
